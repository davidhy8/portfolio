About


Hi, I'm David! I am an data scientist and machine learning engineer with a diverse background in statistics and biology. Equipped with a MSc. in Statistics and a BSc. in Cellular, Molecular and Microbial Biology, I specialize in deriving meaningful and significant insights from biological data. My expertise lies in processing complex and noisy datasets, deriving insights through statistical methods, and predictive modelling with techniques such as machine learning and regression. As a firm believer that biotechnology and artificial intelligence will revolutionize our time, I hope to innovate and build our future with like-minded individuals...

I'm fascinated by artificial intelligence, finance, music, and playing basketball and volleyball
